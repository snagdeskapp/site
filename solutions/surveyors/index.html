Surveyors need a defect record that stands up: photographed, located, and clearly described. Loose photos and scribbled notes don't cut it when the report has your name on it. SnagDesk keeps the evidence and the description together from the moment you spot a defect, so the record is complete before you move to the next room.

A day with SnagDesk

On a defect survey you work through the property recording each issue as you find it. You photograph the crack, mark its extent on the image, tag the location and write the note while the detail is fresh. Nothing is left to be reconstructed later, and none of it depends on a connection.

When the walk is done, the report comes out branded and photographed, with each defect numbered and located. You send it to the client the same day, confident the evidence matches the words.
