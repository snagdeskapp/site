Photo capture & markup

Shoot the defect and draw straight on the photo. Box it, circle it, or arrow to it.

Branded PDF reports

Your company name, logo, prepared-by signature and footer on every page.

Works offline

No account, no signal needed. Everything is stored on your iPhone.

Projects, plots & locations

Group snags by job and location, so nothing gets logged against the wrong room.

How the parts fit together

The features are not a list of separate tools, they are one sequence. You set up a project so everything has somewhere to live. You capture and mark up each defect as you find it. At the end, all of it becomes a branded report in a single tap. Because it runs entirely on your iPhone, none of those steps wait on a connection.

If you want the shorter version, the how it works section on the homepage walks through the same three steps with screenshots.
