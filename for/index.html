Contractors

Close out defects fast and hand over with a clean, photographed record.

Builders

Log snags room by room in the ten seconds you have, without stopping the job.

Site managers

Keep an accurate defect list across every plot, and report on any one of them.

Snagging inspectors

Produce a branded, professional report on every visit, straight from site.

Surveyors

Keep photographed, located defect records where the evidence matches the words.

The same tool, different days

What every one of these roles has in common is that the work happens on foot, often with no signal, and the value only lands when someone else can act on the list. That is why SnagDesk keeps capture, markup and reporting in one place on your phone rather than spreading them across a camera roll, a spreadsheet and an evening at a laptop.
