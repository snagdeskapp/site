For a professional snagging inspector the report is the product. The client is paying for a document they can hand to a developer, and it needs to look credible on the first job and every one after it. SnagDesk turns your on-site walk into a branded, photographed report in seconds, so the thing you deliver matches the fee you charge.

A day with SnagDesk

You arrive at a new-build handover inspection. You set up the project with the client and address, then walk the property methodically. Each defect gets a photo, a markup, a location and a note, in the language a developer will act on. Because it all saves on the phone, the patchy signal in the plot never slows you down.

When you finish, you generate the report. Your company name, your logo and your prepared-by signature sit on every page, with each snag numbered, located and photographed. You email it to the client the same day, looking like the professional outfit you are.
