Snagging software that fits in your pocket

SnagDesk is offline snagging software for UK construction sites. Log every defect with a photo, mark it up, and hand over a branded PDF report, all from your iPhone.

See pricing

Most snagging software was built for a desk. It assumes a laptop, a connection and time at the end of the day to type everything up. On a live construction site none of that is true. You're on your feet, the signal is patchy, and the last thing you want is to redo the whole list at home. SnagDesk is built around how the work actually happens: fast capture on site, and a finished report before you leave.

What snagging software should do on site

The job is simple to describe and easy to get wrong. Find the defect, record it clearly enough that someone else can fix it, and produce a document that holds up with the client. Good snagging software makes each of those steps quick, keeps them linked together, and never gets in the way while you're standing in a half-finished room.

Capture defects without breaking stride

Open a snag, take the photo, mark it up and add the location and a note. It all saves against the right job. There's no separate camera, no loose photos to sort later, and no spreadsheet to reconcile at nine in the evening.

Turn the list into a report in one tap

When the walk is done, SnagDesk builds a branded, multi-page PDF from everything you logged. Your company name and logo sit on every page, each snag appears with its photo and location, and the whole thing is ready to email before you get back to the van.

Made for UK construction
